Born From Quiet Perfection

VELARIS LUX LIVING was founded to create furniture that transcends trend and functions as enduring emotional architecture. Every piece is designed with disciplined proportion, tactile richness, and timeless stillness.

Our ateliers unite heritage craftsmanship with modern precision engineering. From carved frames to hand-finished surfaces, each detail is refined repeatedly until it reflects uncompromising harmony.

For private residences and curated estates, Velaris collections are built to be inherited, not replaced.
